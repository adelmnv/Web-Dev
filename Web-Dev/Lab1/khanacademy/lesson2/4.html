Travel to ...

  1. Hawaii, USA

    Hawaii is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush greenery, and vibrant culture. Indulge in local delicacies like poke and enjoy hula performances under the stars.

      Places to visit

    • Waikiki Beach - Relax on golden sands with a view of Diamond Head.
    • Na Pali Coast - Hike or kayak along this breathtaking coastline.
    • Haleakalā National Park - Witness a sunrise above the clouds.

  2. Kyoto, Japan

    Kyoto offers a perfect blend of ancient traditions and natural beauty. Experience tea ceremonies, visit historic temples, and enjoy the tranquility of Japanese gardens.

      Places to visit

    • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - Admire the shimmering golden temple surrounded by serene gardens.
    • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - Walk through towering bamboo stalks for a magical experience.
    • Fushimi Inari Shrine - Explore thousands of vibrant red torii gates.

  3. Paris, France

    Known as the City of Light, Paris is a haven for art, fashion, and romance. Savor fresh croissants, walk along the Seine, and immerse yourself in the city's vibrant culture.

      Places to visit

    • Eiffel Tower - Climb to the top for panoramic views of the city.
    • Louvre Museum - Marvel at world-famous art, including the Mona Lisa.
    • Montmartre - Stroll through this charming district and see the Sacré-Cœur.
