Pizza Margarita

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 piece
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Fresh mozzarella 200 g
Fresh basil leaves Handful
Olive oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).

Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough and spread tomato sauce evenly over it.

Step 3: Add slices of fresh mozzarella and a few basil leaves on top.

Step 4: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Salad Olivier

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Boiled potatoes 3 medium
Boiled eggs 4
Pickles 4 small
Peas 1 cup
Mayonnaise 2 tablespoons
Sour cream 1/4 cup

Step 1: Dice all vegetables and eggs into small cubes.

Step 2: Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 3: Add mayonnaise and sour cream, mix well. Chill before serving.

Pasta alla Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 80 g
Olive oil 1 1/4 tablespoons
Garlic 1/2 clove
Ham 60 g
Egg yolk 3/4
Grated Parmesan cheese 20 g
10% Cream 40 ml
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Step 1: Boil spaghetti in salted water for 7-10 minutes and drain.

Step 2: Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and lightly brown it.

Step 3: Dice ham and cook with garlic for 5 minutes.

Step 4: Grate Parmesan cheese. Beat egg yolks with cream and salt.

Step 5: Combine spaghetti with garlic and ham in the pan.

Step 6: Add egg mixture and Parmesan, mix well, and cook for 3 minutes.

Step 7: Season with black pepper, garnish with herbs, and serve.
